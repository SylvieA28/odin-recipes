Chicken Sandwich

Description

A crispy, juicy chicken sandwich that tastes just like a Chicken Fil A sandwich.

Ingredients

  • Raw chicken
  • Milk
  • Spices
  • Flour
  • Pickles
  • Buns

Steps

  1. Fillet the chicken
  2. Marinade the chicken fillets for 20 minutes
  3. Cover them in oil and seasoned flour
  4. Fry them for 4-5 minutes or until done
  5. Place the fried chicken and pickles in the buns
