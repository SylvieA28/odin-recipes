Lasagna

A delicious and filling plate of lasagna.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Tomato sauce
  • Meat

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. On top of each lasagna noodle, place meat, mozzarella sauce, and tomato sauce
  3. Bake for 45 minutes
  4. Let sit for 15 minutes
