Pizza

A delicious but healthy pizza for the whole family.

Ingredients

  • Premade Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella sauce
  • Bell peppers
  • Onions
  • Basil
  • Black olives

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 475 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Add a 1/3 cup of tomato and mozzarella sauce
  3. Add all topings besides basil (unless you like it burned)
  4. Bake for 15 minutes or until the crust is brown
